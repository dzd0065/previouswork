The person behind the byline

About Dustin

A journalist’s foundation applied to communications, technical subjects and long-form creative storytelling.

Dustin Duncan is a journalist, communications professional and fiction writer whose career has been built around finding the human meaning inside complicated subjects.

He spent more than five years as a daily newspaper reporter in southern Illinois before continuing his journalism career in Alabama and Georgia. His reporting covered the institutions, decisions and individuals that shaped the communities he served.

He later moved into higher education communications and marketing at Auburn University, where he has produced more than 1,000 stories about engineering, research, faculty expertise, student achievement and institutional impact.

Whether reporting from a city meeting, interviewing an engineering researcher or developing a fictional character, his approach remains the same: listen closely, understand the details and tell the story clearly.
